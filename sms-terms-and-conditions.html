SMS Terms and Conditions — Masjid Al-Wali Informational Text Program

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Program name

Masjid Al-Wali — Caller-requested informational SMS

Program description

This program sends informational SMS messages when a caller to Masjid Al-Wali’s main telephone line asks during the call to receive a text with a link to the information they requested (for example: prayer times PDF, forms, or public program and service pages). Messages are intended to be sent to the phone number that placed the call, in response to that request.

Message frequency

Messages are sent on demand when a caller requests them; overall volume is expected to be low and varies by caller requests.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Check your mobile plan or ask your carrier.
